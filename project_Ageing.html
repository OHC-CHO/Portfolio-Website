Ageing: Real-time simulator of age-related hearing loss

2024.09 - 2024.10 | Python, DSP, Psychoacoustics

Project Overview

Developed a real-time simulator in Python that recreates the psychological experience of age-related hearing loss. Combined auditory-perception research with digital audio techniques—including filtering and the Hilbert transform—to build realistic acoustic degradation models, exploring both the physical and emotional dimensions of hearing impairment.
